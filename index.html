Meat Cutting Yield Calculator

By: Andrew M McCall

A simple no-frills meat cutting cost calculator. This meat yield calculator takes prices per U.S. pound and weights in U.S. pounds and will return the cost and pricing information you need to accurately price your meat cuts. A great training tool for meat apprentices and business tool for managers.
